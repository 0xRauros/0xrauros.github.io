About Me


Hello there, my name is Aritz, let me introduce my self and explain what all this is.

I have decided to open this website for various reasons. The main reason is for not letting go of my passion for technology and keeping myself updated. Practically every day I am reading about new concepts and tinkering with some new technology, so I hope to use this medium to not stop learning. I love reading and talking about topics of my interest, about my passions, about philosophical concepts ... who knows, maybe I will also write about ideas and reflections that come to mind.

I started programming when I was 16 years old, small projects with Arduino and very basic exercises in Python. Little by little I got a little deeper into the world of programming and decided to study a 2-year degree when I finished high school. When I finished my studies, I did an internship in my first company as a programmer. We developed a library using Python as the base technology and a desktop application to automate internal processes of the company. After that I started to work for another company where I developed desktop applications with VisualBasic .NET and MySQL mainly.

After the global pandemic and a series of existential doubts that I felt I had to solve, I decided to take a break and get out of the wheel of the system for a while. Inspired by adventure stories and letting myself be carried away a bit by the romanticism of life, I undertook a small trip in which I lived incredible experiences. From the verdant landscapes of Austria, the gloomy cloudy sunrises of Transylvania, to the imposing Acropolis of Athens and the wild and revealing desert of Wadi Rum in Jordan.

Now that I'm back I feel ready to face the days again with motivation and enthusiasm, keep learning and growing.

In short, this is a personal space in which I plan to publish posts, projects and some other thing that I feel like. I appreciate that you have read this far.

Aritz
