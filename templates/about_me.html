About me


Hello there!

I'm Aritz, a technology geek with a passion for learning.

I've been programming since high school and I've always been amazed by the creative freedom and endless knowledge of this discipline. There is always something new to learn, something to teach and something to contribute.

Currently I'm more focused on backend web development, but I always find time to tinker with some other technology for pure pleasure. Sometimes I like tinkering with software defined radio systems , RFID and NFC systems. Nothing like a rainy Sunday afternoon with an NFC reader, your passport and some Python to have fun ;)

In my free time I like to surf whenever I can, read books of all kinds, from technical essays to novels (I have a special weakness for fantasy ones) and travel, I love getting lost with my backpack in a new place.

Although the night makes me feel a very deep peace, the truth is that I am a morning bird. I wake up at 5:00 AM and after meditating for a while to clear my head, I enjoy about an hour of reading and coffee. Then I go for a run and take a cold shower afterward. Then I'm ready to start work or whatever I have to do while fresh as a rose.
